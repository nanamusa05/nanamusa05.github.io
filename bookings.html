WHO WE ARE

HAPPA (Horses and Ponies Protection Association), we are a dedicated team of animal welfare advocates committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming horses and ponies in need, while tirelessly working to prevent cruelty and promote compassionate care for equines across the UK.
