About Us 

At HAPPA, we are dedicated to the rehabilitation and protection of horses and ponies across the UK. Our team works tirelessly to ensure the safety and well-being of these majestic animals through rescue, rehoming, and educational programs. We believe that every horse and pony deserves a second chance, and we strive to educate the public on responsible animal ownership.

Our Mission

At the Horses and Ponies Protection Association (HAPPA), our mission is to ensure the well-being and safety of equines across the UK. We are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming horses and ponies in need, while advocating for their rights and promoting responsible care. With a deep commitment to preventing cruelty, we work tirelessly to raise awareness about equine welfare, educate the public on compassionate care practices, and build a supportive community where horses and ponies are valued and respected.
